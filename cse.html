COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

About the Department
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was established in 1991 with an intake of 40 students for B.Tech. program. Over the years, it has grown significantly, with the intake increased to 300 by 2021. The department also offers M.Tech. in Computer Science and Technology (CST) since 2006.

Infrastructure and Faculty
The department boasts all necessary infrastructure including 11 laboratories, 3 research labs, and 2 seminar halls. With 62 faculty members, including 13 doctorates, the department ensures a high-quality teaching-learning experience. Faculty members are actively engaged in research in various areas such as Cryptography, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, and more.

Research and Consultancy
The department has secured projects worth Rs. 1.68 crores funded by various agencies and institutions. A total of 581 research papers have been published, demonstrating the department's commitment to research.

Placement and Student Activities
The department has an impressive placement record, with 781 offers made in the academic year 2021-2022, including a highest package of Rs. 47.2 lakhs per annum. The department encourages student participation in professional bodies and organizes various co-curricular and extracurricular activities.

Future Initiatives
The department continually updates its curriculum to meet industry demands, introducing courses in emerging areas such as Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, and Internet of Things. It also offers Honors & Minors programs for bright students.

  • 13 faculty members hold Doctorate degrees, and 30 are registered for Ph.D.
  • The average experience of Professors and Associate Professors is about 20 years, while Assistant Professors have an average of 15 years.

  • Graduates of the program are equipped with both basic and specialized knowledge in computer science and engineering, along with skills in mathematics and modern technologies.
